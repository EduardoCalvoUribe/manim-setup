# FAQ: Where Can I Learn More About Manim's Internal Structure?

> Source: https://docs.manim.community/en/stable/faq/internals.html

Efforts to document the internal structure of Manim are ongoing on the [developer wiki](https://github.com/ManimCommunity/manim/wiki/Developer-documentation-%28WIP%29).

Keep in mind that since this is a work in progress, the information you find may be incomplete, outdated, or even wrong. Still, it should serve as a good starting point. The wiki is open for anyone to edit — feel free to add information or even questions directly on the wiki pages.

For a structured deep-dive, see also the [deep_dive guide](../guides/deep_dive.md), which walks through the Cairo renderer's internals including mobject architecture, scene management, and the render loop.
